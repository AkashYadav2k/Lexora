What is Lexora?

Lexora (pronounced /lek-soh-rah/) comes from the Latin word lex, meaning law. Born from the belief that law should be understood by everyone, Lexora simplifies complex legal systems using AI. It transforms legal complexity into confidence — helping you understand your rights, find reliable answers, and make meaningful decisions effortlessly.
